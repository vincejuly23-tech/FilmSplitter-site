FilmSplitter Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

Overview

FilmSplitter is a local image processing app for splitting scanned film strips into individual photos. The app processes images selected by the user on device.

Data We Do Not Collect

FilmSplitter does not collect analytics, tracking data, account information, location data, contacts, or user content on a server.

FilmSplitter does not sell user data and does not use third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.

Photos and Files

FilmSplitter can import images that you select from Photos, Files, or another app's share sheet. These images are used only to detect frames, preview crop boxes, edit crop areas, rotate frames, and export results.

Exported photos are saved to your Photos library only after you grant permission.

Drafts

FilmSplitter may store recent local file references and editing state on your device so you can restore recent work. Drafts are stored locally and are not uploaded to a server.

Purchases

FilmSplitter Pro is processed through Apple's in-app purchase system. FilmSplitter does not receive or store your payment card information.
