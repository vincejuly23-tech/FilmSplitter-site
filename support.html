FilmSplitter Support

FilmSplitter helps film photography users split scanned film strips into individual photos. The app supports importing user-selected images, reviewing detected frames, adjusting crop boxes, rotating frames, and exporting results to Photos.

Common Questions

Does FilmSplitter require an account?
No. FilmSplitter does not require a user account.

Where are images processed?
Images selected by the user are processed locally on device.

Where are exported photos saved?
Exported photos are saved to the user's Photos library after permission is granted.
